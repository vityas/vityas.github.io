The Big Question

Does review matter?

Let's imagine you are selling items on Amazon, and just got a very negative review. This is unfortunate, but how much does it really hurt your bottom line? And more importantly, what aspects of review make it matter. Or it is just the rating itself.

Lets dive into this question in a little more detail

How it begins

First we need data. The more the merrier. Lets focus on Amazon for now. We need to get reviews and corresponding prices.

Reviews

Instead of scarpping amazon website there is good amount of data available here for research purposes. It is rovided by Julian McAuley at UCSD.

It consists of millions of reviews and their metadata for as long as 18 years ending in mid 2014; Data is separated into 24 categories (e.g. Books. Electronics, Beauty, Sport).
